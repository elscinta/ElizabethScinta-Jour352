Facts About Netflix

Most people around the world have Netflix subscriptions, myself included. I've created a chart below that shows how many minutes subscribers are spending watching three shows, The Office, Friends and Stranger Things.

I've also included another chart that shows how many episodes are a part of each of those shows. This shows that Stranger Things has much fewer episodes than both The Office and Friends so it makes sense that people spend less time watching it.

Finally, I've created a map that shows where Netflix subscribers are in the world and which region has the most. North America and Canada have the most subscribers with 69 million.
